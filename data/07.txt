The Media Portrayals of Sexuality and Its Effects Essay discusses how media portrayals of sexuality affect people's behaviors and perceptions of sex and sexuality, including negative emotional experiences for women due to pervasive objectification, perpetuation of negative stereotypes, and promotion of sexual activity among the youth. The paper argues that the saturation of media with sexuality can have a positive impact on human development and promotes sexual health, change inattitude towards non-heterosexual sexual behaviors, and creation of sexual awareness. The media practice model suggests that the more time a person spends consuming media, the more likely they are to accept its fictional images as reality.
